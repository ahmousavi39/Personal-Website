About me

My name is Amirhossein and I'm 15 years old, I'm from Afghanistan but unfortunately, because there are many problems in Afghanistan I'm not able to live in my country, and I'm currently living in Germany as a refugee student.

I'm currently in 10th class, I have experienced many different educational systems, because I attended school in 5 different countries (Afghanistan, Iran, Turkey, Greece and Germany), I study the 1st class on Afghanistan, and after facing many difficulties I forced to leave Afghanistan and I went to Iran, I study 4 classes in Iran, 2 classes in Turkey and after that 3 years in Greece. of course, it was not optional and I didn't choose it and I faced many difficulties in these years, but I learned many new things on this long migration like different cultures, many new languages, different people, and ...

I'm a programmer and i started learning programming from Social Hackers Academy.
I love programming and my favorite part of programming is Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence and I hope I would be able to learn them very soon!

Now, after arriving in my final targeted country, where I have access to education and able to grow, I'm trying to help other refugees, because they are in a really difficult situation and I understand them ! You may ask yourself that how can a refugee of my age can help others,
but I believe that I have big power, and my power is programming and my responsibility, I feel that helping other refugees and people is my human duty, and I should be able to help them.
